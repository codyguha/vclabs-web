About

At VC Labs we are inspired by the promise of communities. Communities are the way we connect, the way we learn, and the way we collectively drive positive action. Communities are powerful and when used effectively can add tremendous value to any modern agile business.

Getting the people together and talking is only half the battle. The real key to unlocking the success of any community is to focus on building a healthy community. More than ever it is absolutely critical to have active, engaged members that feel they have an appreciated and valued voice. Communities that are engaged, well informed, and active are the communities that people want to be a part of. At VC labs we recognize that all communities benefit from more engaged members and that engagement can be achieved by leveraging frictionless technologies - both existing and emerging.

Think of VC labs as your technology partner. Work smarter and get the most out of your communities - together we will do amazing things.

Mission

At VC Labs we strive to develop products and services that boost the value of communities. We build solutions that appeal to both community members and administrators. Good design is at the heart of our culture, and we take enormous pride in balancing the utility of a solution with the usability, and desirability that our partners expect. We strive to develop and deliver offerings that will rapidly amplify the membership experience and ultimately make your community a more effective and valuable tool.

Focus Areas

The following themes and topics currently drive innovation at the lab.

Community Inputs

  1. Conversing with community members via popular messaging apps - Facebook Messenger, KiK, iMessage, Wechat, etc.
  2. Next generation surveys via conversational user interfaces - text and voice based chat bots, for example
  3. Value added insights through data aggregation - member data merged with CRM data, for example

Member Engagement

  1. Enhancing the community onboarding experience through fun and engaging survey challenges.
  2. Bringing gamification and other joy-of-use mechanics to communities.
  3. Enabling more robust member conversations through consistent sharebacks - e.g. status updates on the implication of insights gleaned from surveys

Stakeholder Value Adds

  1. Integrations with common social business platforms - Slack, Microsoft Teams, Saleforce Chatter, etc
  2. Next generation stakeholder portals
  3. Next generation project and activity management platforms
  4. Next generation dashboard and analysis tools

Take Ten

“Take Ten” was envisioned as “Module 1” in a series of value added “Survey Challenges” that an IC manager might turn on for their community. The challenges themselves are optional and meant to exist as experiences that the user can choose to opt into.

From a user experience perspective, Take Ten was designed to be cognitively simple, aestheticlly pleasing, and fun. Our intent was to directly address the community onboarding engagement gap, and our goal was to deliver a content experience that would make joining a new community more inspiring.

The screenshots below give an overview of the apps interaction model while providing a general sense of the overall Take Ten experience.

10 questions a day for 10 days
10 questions a day for 10 days A key utility promise for Take Ten is its ability to establish a simple but consistent interaction pattern that brings an IC member back in touch with the community on a regular basis. Each daily question set is presented around a simple theme. Daily question sets begin with a clear introduction and end with a satisfying reward that communicates success and completion. The experience is meant to be short but consistently positive and fun.
